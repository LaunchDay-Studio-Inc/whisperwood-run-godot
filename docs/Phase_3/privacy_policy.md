## Overview
Whisperwood Run ("the Game") is developed by LaunchDay Studio Inc. This privacy policy explains what data the Game collects and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

### Local Data (Offline)
- **Save data**: Game progress, scores, currency, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
- **Analytics events**: Gameplay events (run completion, quest progress, etc.) are logged locally in a JSON file on your device. This data is **not** transmitted to any server by default.

### Optional Online Data (Android only)
- **Advertising**: If you have not purchased "Remove Ads," the Game uses Google AdMob to display rewarded and interstitial ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data per [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We request **non-personalized ads** by default.
- **In-App Purchases**: Purchase transactions are processed through Google Play. We do not store your payment information.

### Web Build (itch.io)
- The web build does **not** include any advertising SDK.
- No data is transmitted to external servers.
- All save data is stored in browser local storage.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No location data
- No contacts or address book access
- No camera or microphone access
- No cross-app tracking

## Children's Privacy
The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Game does not contain social features, chat, or user-generated content.

## Data Retention
- Local save data persists until you uninstall the Game or clear app data.
- Analytics logs are stored locally and can be deleted by clearing app data.

## Your Rights
- You can delete all local data by clearing the app's data/cache in your device settings.
- You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings.
- You can purchase "Remove Ads" to eliminate all interstitial advertisements.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be noted with an updated "Last Updated" date.
